About:

Necessitas is a teaching application written in JavaScript that works with the Clotho 3.0 JavaScript API to demonstrate Clotho's capabilities.

Where to Start:

If you are completely new to Clotho, we recommend starting with the general use tutorial. If you would like to continue and develop your own application for Clotho, the developer tutorial will show you the basics.

Necessitas:

She is the Roman godess of fate, inevitability, and necessity; she marks the creation of the cosmos. Her Greek equivalent is Ananke. She is also the mother of the three fates, Nona, Decima, and Morta.
